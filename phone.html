The Sound

Rooted in experience. Open to what comes next.

Laura Goodstone’s music blends the emotional weight of soul, the edge of rock, the honesty of country and the depth of blues. Her songs are built around real moments: change, strength, survival, reflection and the search for connection.

With new music connected to Nashville sessions and a growing body of original work, Laura is shaping a sound that feels both grounded and forward-moving — intimate enough to feel personal, wide enough for listeners to find themselves inside the story.

Biography

A voice built on story, strength and soul.

Born in Argentina and now building her path in the United States, Laura Goodstone brings together a wide range of influences — rock, country, blues and soul — into a sound that is emotionally driven and deeply human.

For Laura, songwriting is closely tied to lived experience. Her work reflects challenge, growth and reflection, but it is equally focused on connection: creating songs where listeners can recognize pieces of their own lives.
