Biography

A voice built on story, strength and soul.

Born in Argentina and now building her path in the United States, Laura Goodstone has spent years developing her voice through live performance, drawing from a wide range of influences that include rock, country, blues and soul.

That blend now defines her sound: emotionally driven, grounded in storytelling, and carried by a strong, expressive vocal style.

The Sound

A sound shaped by country, rock, blues and soul — emotionally driven, grounded in storytelling, and carried by a voice built through years on stage.

New Music

She’s Country

Her upcoming release “She’s Country” marks the beginning of this next chapter. The track introduces a perspective that feels both personal and universal, centered on strength, resilience and identity.

Songwriting

Music tied to lived experience.

For Laura, songwriting is closely tied to lived experience. Her work reflects moments of challenge, growth and reflection, but it is equally focused on connection: creating songs that listeners can see themselves in.

That balance between personal storytelling and broader relatability sits at the core of what she is building as an artist.

Direction

Forward

Next Chapter

Nashville, new songs, and what comes next.

She continues to evolve creatively through ongoing work connected to Nashville, where she returns to record and develop new material. Those sessions are helping shape a larger body of work, with multiple releases planned throughout the year as she moves toward an upcoming EP.

A follow-up single, “I’m Still Alive,” continues that direction, pulling from a deeply personal experience that reshaped her outlook on both life and music.

Live Performer

The stage remains part of the identity.

Beyond recording, Laura Goodstone’s background as a live performer remains a key part of her identity. The stage is where her songs fully come to life, and that energy carries into the way she writes and records.
