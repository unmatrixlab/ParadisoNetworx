🤖 What is a Telegram Bot?

Your Vision Core security system is entirely controlled through a Telegram Bot — an automated virtual assistant built directly into the Telegram messaging app.

Instead of downloading a separate app or memorizing complex codes, you simply tap the intuitive buttons integrated into your chat to arm the system, configure AI algorithms, mask zones, and receive instant 4K alerts.

👇 Try it out: Tap the interactive buttons on the simulated phone screen below to discover what each feature does and how to configure it.

🔐 Access Control & Permissions

1. Claiming Ownership: The very first person to send a message (like /start) to the newly installed bot automatically becomes the Super Admin.

2. Adding Users: To grant access to family or staff, simply ask them to find your bot on Telegram and send it any message (e.g., "hello").

3. Admin Approval: The bot will automatically block their initial attempt and send a private alert to the Admin with an ✅ APPROVE ACCESS button. Tap it to permanently authorize them.
