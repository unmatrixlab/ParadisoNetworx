Artist Identity

Laura Goodstone

Laura Goodstone is a country rock artist, songwriter, performer, and vocal coach building a bold, modern identity that fuses country storytelling with the edge of classic rock. With a powerful stage presence and emotionally driven vocals, she delivers high-energy live shows across the U.S. while developing original music connected to Nashville. Her performances are direct, engaging, and built to connect with real audiences—from live music venues to festival stages—combining authenticity, energy, and a clear artistic voice that stands out immediately.

The Sound

Rooted in experience. Open to what comes next.

Laura Goodstone’s music blends the weight of modern country storytelling, the edge of rock, the passion of soul, and the depth of blues. Her songs are built around real moments: change, strength, survival, reflection, and the search for connection.

With new music connected to Nashville sessions and a growing body of original work, Laura is shaping a sound that feels both grounded and forward-moving — intimate enough to feel personal, wide enough for listeners to find themselves inside the story.
