Magic 13

Paradiso Networx redesigned the sound system at Magic 13 to deliver exceptional audio quality and elevate the live performance experience. This upgrade has significantly enhanced the entertainment value for both audiences and performers.

Project Overview

Magic 13, a dynamic live venue in Miami, aimed to improve its acoustic environment and overall show quality. Paradiso Networx implemented a high-performance audio system tailored to the venue’s unique layout, ensuring clear, immersive sound throughout the space.

Services Provided

  • Sound System: Installed a fully redesigned sound system optimized for live performances, featuring advanced audio components and precise acoustic calibration.
  • Point of Sale: Installed modern POS systems, integrating user-friendly interfaces to streamline transactions and improve service efficiency.

Key Benefits

  • Exceptional sound clarity and coverage enhancing the audience’s live experience.
  • Elevated production quality supporting a wider range of performers.
  • Increased venue appeal through professional-grade audio infrastructure.
