12 English Tenses with work

Affirmative ✅ • Negative ❌ • Question ❓ — with read-aloud buttons



Tip: Click the 🔊 button to hear the sentence or the tense title for a detailed explanation.

1) Present Simple

habit / fact
✅ I work every day.
❌ I don’t work on Sundays.
❓ Do you work here?

2) Present Continuous

now / in progress
✅ I am working right now.
❌ I am not working at the moment.
❓ Are you working today?

3) Present Perfect

past → present
✅ I have worked here since 2010.
❌ I haven’t worked there before.
❓ Have you worked in this company?

4) Present Perfect Continuous

duration
✅ I have been working all morning.
❌ I haven’t been working much lately.
❓ Have you been working hard today?

5) Past Simple

finished past
✅ I worked yesterday.
❌ I didn’t work last night.
❓ Did you work last week?

6) Past Continuous

past in progress
✅ I was working when you called.
❌ I wasn’t working at that time.
❓ Were you working last night?

7) Past Perfect

past before past
✅ I had worked before I moved here.
❌ I hadn’t worked in that field before.
❓ Had you worked there before the crisis?

8) Past Perfect Continuous

duration before past
✅ I had been working for two hours when she arrived.
❌ I hadn’t been working long before the meeting.
❓ Had you been working all day before you left?

9) Future Simple (will)

prediction / decision
✅ I will work tomorrow.
❌ I won’t work next Sunday.
❓ Will you work next week?

10) Future Continuous

future in progress
✅ I will be working at 8 pm.
❌ I won’t be working at that time.
❓ Will you be working late tonight?

11) Future Perfect

completed by a time
✅ I will have worked 10 hours by 6 pm.
❌ I won’t have worked enough by then.
❓ Will you have worked 40 hours by Friday?

12) Future Perfect Continuous

duration to a time
✅ I will have been working for 5 years by next June.
❌ I won’t have been working here that long.
❓ Will you have been working here for 10 years by 2030?
